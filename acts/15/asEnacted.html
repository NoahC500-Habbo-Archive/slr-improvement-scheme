C1What follows is the act as enacted, without any revisions applied. As such the text on this page is not necessarily statute. See here for the current copy.

1Positions

1The following ranks shall make up the senate;

1Speaker of the House of Representatives (4iC)

2House Majority Leader (5iC)

3House Minority Leader (5iC)

4House Majority Whip (6iC)

5House Minority Whip (6iC)

6Clerk of the House of Representatives (7iC)

7Parliamentarian of the House of Representatives (7iC)

8Sergeant-at-Atms of the House of Representatives (7iC)

9Majority Representative (8iC)

10Minority Representative (9iC)

2Each rank shall hold one person, except for;

1Majority Representative – Maximum of 16

2Minority Representative – Maximum of 30

3Specifics

1Each rank shall be assigned duties by the Speaker of the House of Representatives, and by legislation

2Each individual in the Legislature shall be assigned a committee in accordance with the Legislation Act 2025
